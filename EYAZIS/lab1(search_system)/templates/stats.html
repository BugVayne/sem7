BM25 Formula
score(D, Q) = Σ[IDF(term) × (f(term, D) × (k1 + 1)) / (f(term, D) + k1 × (1 - b + b × (|D| / avgdl)))]

Where:
• f(term, D): frequency of term in document D
• |D|: length of document D (number of words)
• avgdl: average document length in collection
• k1, b: parameters (k1=2.0, b=0.75)
• IDF(term) = log((N - n(term) + 0.5) / (n(term) + 0.5) + 1)
